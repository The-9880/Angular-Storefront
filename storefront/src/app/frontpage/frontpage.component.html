Welcome to the Fruit Store!

This is a web application that simulates a fruit shop. As an unauthenticated user, you can browse the catalog of items available. To get started with purchasing and saving item lists, you should click the login button below to create an account. Some users with higher privileges - store managers - are capable of accessing a front-end control panel interface to make changes to the underlying database or just moderate the websites social features (comments and the like). They are also able to exercise administrative privileges over the accounts of other users to help them moderate.
